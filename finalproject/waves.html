Waves 1-62 Strategy

The picture above is a map of the fight cave. The green circles mark where an enemy could spawn and the labelled rocks are potential safe spots. The most common spot used is Italy Rock as it is very easy to trap a melee enemy on the walls. There are monsters that can drain your prayer, leaving you vulnerable. Another splits into two more monsters when it dies, one that attacks with range, one that hits very hard with melee and can heal, and the last one attacks with magic. As the waves progress more and more monsters appear and the player has to know which to attack at the right time.
