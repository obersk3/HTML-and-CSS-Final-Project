Overview

In October of 2005 Jagex, the developer of RuneScape, created a new mini game called the TzHaar Fight Cave. It is a combat minigame where the player must face off against waves and waves of progressively challenging enemies to get to the final boss, TzTok-Jad on wave 63. The player must stay in the minigame until the final boss or they have to start over, there is no time to resupply. There are many different builds a player could come up with to defeat the boss, the most common builds include a very high Ranged level as well as at least level 43 Prayer for protection prayers.
